Support Desk Playbook

Use this playbook whenever a JIRA ticket is in Acknowledgement status.

D1 Active
👤 ...

This playbook is to be used by the Support Desk Agent when a JIRA ticket is in Acknowledgement status.

Step-by-Step Playbook

Step 1: Change Status

Change the ticket status from Acknowledgement to Analysis and Study.

Step 2: Update Current User

Update the Current User field with your agent name so ticket ownership is clear.

Step 3: Read the Customer Ask

Read the full ticket carefully. Understand what the customer has asked and review all comments.

Step 4: Review Attachments

Review every attachment, screenshot, and supporting file attached to the ticket.

Step 5: Confirm Whether Details Are Sufficient

Check whether the details provided are enough to review or reproduce the reported issue.

"Say what all information is required if the BL is not listed in the WAR."
Step 6: Ask Luna About the Issue

Ask Luna about the issue submitted by the customer using the clearest possible context.

Step 7: Update JIRA Fields While Luna Is Processing

Update: SVM Incharge, Components, HD Solution. Under HD Solution: Question raised to BOT and Luna Response.

Step 8: Understand the Response and Perform Analysis

Read Luna's response carefully and fill the Analysis field. Always put your name at the bottom.

Step 9: Take the Next Action

Based on your analysis, assign the ticket to the customer or engineering team as needed.

Step 10: Escalate High-Urgency Tickets

For Show Stopper and Critical tickets, use the defined escalation process without delay.

Golden Rules

  • Always read the full ticket before acting.
  • Always review attachments.
  • Never skip HD Solution updates.
  • Validate Luna response before using it.
  • Always write clear analysis and include your name.
  • Do not delay escalation for critical issues.
